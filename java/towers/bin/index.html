Tower Game

Last Updated 12/12/2007

Objective

Sort your tower in ascending order before the Computer does.

Legend

  • Computer AI tower (Left)
  • Your tower (Right)
  • Card Deck (middle)

Gameplay

Each turn you may do one of the following:

  1. Place middle card in your tower
  2. Draw card

You must remove a card in your tower to make room for middle card.

Cards removed from a tower are placed on TOP of the deck.

You may Draw card more than once per turn.
